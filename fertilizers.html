Official guidance for safe and effective fertilizer usage

Chemical Fertilizers

Chemical fertilizers are manufactured fertilizers that supply essential nutrients such as Nitrogen (N), Phosphorus (P), and Potassium (K).

  • Used for quick crop growth
  • Includes Urea, DAP, NPK
  • Should be applied as per soil test

Natural / Organic Fertilizers

Natural fertilizers improve soil fertility using organic matter such as compost, green manure, and bio-fertilizers.

  • Improves long-term soil health
  • Eco-friendly and sustainable
  • Recommended under organic farming schemes
